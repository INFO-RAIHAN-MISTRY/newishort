Frequently Asked Questions

Why do we use URL shorteners?

To convert long URLs in to short URLs. Using a short link with URL shorteners you can track the analytics of the link i.e about the traffic generated through the link.

What are Benefits of a short URL?

If you’re working in the digital space, chances are you’ve encountered URL shorteners but may not have taken a deeper dive into their many benefits. By using a URL shortener, it’s possible to make long and complex URLs easier to share with your followers without losing any of the original hyperlink data.

What is a QR Code?

It is a machine-scannable image that can instantly be read using a Smartphone camera. Every QR code consists of a number of black squares and dots which represent certain pieces of information. When your Smartphone scans this code, it translate that information into something that can be easily understand by humans.

What is a custom URL shortener?

A custom, or branded URL shortener, is when you've connected your own custom domain to a URL shortener which acts as a base for all the short links you create.
